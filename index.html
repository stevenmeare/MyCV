About Me

Hi! My name is Steve, and I'm a ski instructor trainer, indie game developer, and aspiring professional software engineer! I have a passion for people-facing roles and solving tricky, technical problems. I'm always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow.

Having moved out of the ski industry during the COVID-19 pandemic, I have been working in administration and project support roles. In these roles, I have been able to apply skills developed in my hobbyist software development projects to automate and streamline processes, saving time and reducing errors. I am now looking to transition into a full-time software development role, where I can continue to learn and grow as a developer.

I am currently completing a Software Engineering Bootcamp with HyperionDev and the University of Manchester. The course covers a wide range of topics, including Python, SQL, HTML, and CCS. I am also working on a number of personal projects, including a high-performance procedural terrain generation system for Unity, and a web-based project management tool.
